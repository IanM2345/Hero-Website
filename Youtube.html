Youtube career

Olajide Olayinka Williams "JJ" Olatunji (born 19 June 1993), known professionally as KSI, is an English Internet personality, professional boxer and musician. He is the CEO of Misfits Boxing and the co-owner of Prime Hydration, XIX Vodka, Sidemen Clothing, and restaurant chain Sides. He registered his main YouTube channel in 2009 and has since built a following posting gaming commentary videos of the FIFA video game series. His content later diversified to include vlog and comedy-style videos. As of October 2022, he has over 41 million subscribers and over 10 billion video views across his three YouTube channels.

KSIs Main Channel

KSI, a YouTuber, has gained immense popularity since its inception in July 2009. The channel, which has a vast audience, features a variety of content including gaming, vlogs, music, challenges, and more. Initially, KSI gained fame through his FIFA gameplay videos. As the channel grew, he expanded to include vlogs, challenges, and comedy sketches, providing viewers with insight into his personal life and interactions. His music career has also gained significant attention, with numerous singles, music videos, and collaborations. KSI's involvement in boxing matches, particularly high-profile bouts against fellow YouTubers like Joe Weller and Logan Paul, has been a significant part of his content. KSI frequently collaborates with other YouTubers, showcasing their camaraderie and chemistry. His engagement with his audience is a key aspect of his content. As of January 2022, KSI's main YouTube channel has over 20 million subscribers, billions of views, a consistent upload schedule, high engagement levels, and influence beyond YouTube. His involvement in boxing matches, music releases, and collaborations with other prominent figures further solidifies his status as a cultural phenomenon.

SECOND CHANNEL

KSI's second YouTube channel, "JJ Olatunji," offers a more intimate look into his life beyond his main content. Both channels contribute to KSI's overall presence and influence within the YouTube community.
