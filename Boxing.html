KSI VS JOE WELLER

The KSI vs. Joe Weller fight was a highly anticipated boxing match between two prominent British YouTubers, KSI (Olajide "JJ" Olatunji Jr.) and Joe Weller. The match took place on February 3, 2018, at the Copper Box Arena in London, United Kingdom. It was one of the first major events in the world of YouTube boxing and garnered significant attention from fans and media alike. Leading up to the fight, there was a considerable amount of buildup and trash talk between KSI and Joe Weller, both of whom had large followings on their respective YouTube channels. The rivalry between them intensified as they exchanged insults and challenges through social media and YouTube videos. The fight itself was streamed live on YouTube and attracted a massive online audience. KSI ultimately emerged victorious, defeating Joe Weller by technical knockout (TKO) in the third round. Throughout the match, KSI demonstrated his superior boxing skills and athleticism, overpowering Weller with aggressive punches and combinations.

KSI VS LOGAN PAUL1

The first boxing match between KSI (Olajide "JJ" Olatunji Jr.) and Logan Paul was a highly anticipated event in the world of YouTube and boxing. It took place on August 25, 2018, at the Manchester Arena in Manchester, United Kingdom. Leading up to the fight, there was immense hype and buildup, with both KSI and Logan Paul engaging in extensive trash talk and promotional activities to drum up interest. The match garnered significant attention from fans and media worldwide due to the massive followings of both YouTubers. The fight itself was a six-round amateur boxing match, with no headgear worn by the competitors. The match ended in a majority draw, with one judge scoring the bout in favor of KSI, another in favor of Logan Paul, and the third judge scoring it as a draw. The draw result led to speculation about a potential rematch to determine a definitive winner.

KSI VS LOGAN PAUL 2

The second boxing match between KSI (Olajide "JJ" Olatunji Jr.) and Logan Paul took place on November 9, 2019, at the Staples Center in Los Angeles, California. This rematch was highly anticipated following their first bout, which ended in a majority draw. Similar to the first fight, there was significant hype and buildup leading up to the rematch, with both KSI and Logan Paul engaging in intense training and promotional activities to generate interest among fans and media. The rematch was promoted as a professional boxing match, with both fighters making their professional boxing debuts. The second fight was a closely contested six-round bout, with both KSI and Logan Paul showcasing improved boxing skills and strategies compared to their first encounter. However, KSI ultimately emerged victorious, defeating Logan Paul by split decision. Two of the judges scored the bout 57-54 and 56-55 in favor of KSI, while the third judge scored it 56-55 in favor of Logan Paul.

KSI TWO FIGHTS ONE NIGHT PART1

After an injury forced Alex Wassabi out of a meeting with KSI, the YouTuber decided to up the ante and take on two opponents in one night. He kicked off the first installment of the DAZN X Series 001 PPV by dominating rapper Swarmz before finishing him in the second round.The night’s first match marked KSI’s official return to influencer boxing. Shortly after the first bell rang, KSI jumped onto the offensive. Taking command of the ring and repeatedly forcing Swarmz into the corner. Despite being the heavy underdog, Swarmz managed to survive the first round despite being knocked down early into the match. KSI managed to land another knockdown strike onto Swarmz seconds into the second round, though he failed to get back up this time. With his impressive two-round TKO, KSI won his first match back into the boxing scene with plenty of time to prepare for his second bout of the night.

KSI VS PINEDA TWO FIGHTS ONE NIGHT

KSI defeated Luis Alcaraz Pineda via a third-round TKO (:50). The fight began with KSI opening with a right hand, and Pineda continued to complain. The referee's poor decision led to the fight ending. In round 2, KSI responded with a hard body shot, and it became a seek-and-destroy match. Pineda landed a jab but was on his bicycle. KSI then dropped Pineda again, and he landed a left hook and a short right hand. KSI continued to push-ups, but the fight continued. KSI continued to tear into Pineda's body and punch to the head, which Pineda complained about. The unofficial scorecard for the fight was 10-8, with KSI (20-15). In round 1, KSI started hot and went to the body, dropping Pineda with an overhand right. Pineda complained about being hit behind the head and was checked out. KSI continued to drop Pineda, and he took a knee and complained again. The fight was a brutal and frustrating experience for both KSI and Pineda.

KSI VS TOMMY FURY

Tommy Fury's decision win over YouTuber KSI has been altered after an incorrect scorecard was added up. Fury, half-brother of world heavyweight boxing champion Tyson, was a majority-decision winner against KSI in Manchester, with two scorecards reading 57-56 in Fury's favor. However, the 57-57 scorecard from judge Rafael Ramos was the result of incorrect maths, and Fury was a unanimous-decision victor. Fury remained unbeaten as a professional boxer with the win, despite being docked a point for illegal shots to KSI's head. KSI, who is known as Olajide Olatunji, mocked Fury after the fight, stating that Fury had to win to be considered a boxer.
