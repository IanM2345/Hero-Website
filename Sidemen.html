The Sidemen

The Sidemen are a British YouTube group consisting of several YouTubers who collaborate on various content, including gaming, challenges, and vlogs. KSI is one of the founding members of the group, which also includes other popular YouTubers such as Miniminter, Zerkaa, Behzinga, Vikkstar123, TBJZL (Tobi), and W2S (Wroetoshaw)

MINIMINTER (SIMON EDWARD MINTER)

Miniminter shares a close friendship with KSI, having been one of the founding members of the Sidemen alongside him. Their friendship is built on years of collaboration in gaming, challenges, and vlogs, where they often display their banter and camaraderie. They frequently support each other in their individual endeavors, demonstrating a strong bond beyond their shared content creation.

ZERKAA (JOSH BRADELY)

Zerkaa's friendship with KSI dates back to the formation of the Sidemen group, where they've collaborated on numerous gaming and challenge videos. Their relationship is characterized by mutual respect and camaraderie, as they often support each other's projects and engage in friendly banter. Their shared history within the Sidemen cements their bond as close friends and collaborators.
